Since our founding, Haifa Medical Center has set the standard for healthcare delivery, blending pioneering medical advancements with a deep commitment to the well-being of our patients. Our prestigious ranking reflects our unwavering dedication to quality, safety, and innovation, making us a trusted destination for patients from around the globe.

Our Departments

Haifa Medical Center is home to a comprehensive array of specialized departments, each equipped with the latest technology and staffed by leaders in their fields. Our multidisciplinary approach ensures that every patient receives personalized, holistic care tailored to their unique needs. Key departments include:

Cardiology and Cardiovascular Surgery: Our world-renowned heart center features advanced diagnostic tools, minimally invasive procedures, and a team of cardiologists and surgeons who pioneer life-saving techniques, from complex heart transplants to robotic-assisted surgeries.

Oncology: At the forefront of cancer care, our oncology department offers cutting-edge therapies, including precision medicine, immunotherapy, and radiation technology, supported by a compassionate team dedicated to improving outcomes and quality of life.

Neurology and Neurosurgery: Equipped with state-of-the-art neuroimaging and robotic surgical systems, our neurology team treats conditions ranging from stroke and epilepsy to brain tumors and spinal disorders with unparalleled expertise.

Orthopedics: Specializing in joint replacement, sports medicine, and spinal surgery, our orthopedic department combines innovative techniques with rehabilitation programs to restore mobility and enhance recovery.

Pediatrics: Our pediatric wing provides specialized care for children, from neonatal intensive care to pediatric oncology, delivered in a nurturing environment designed to support young patients and their families.

Transplant Center: A leader in organ transplantation, we offer expertise in kidney, liver, heart, and lung transplants, supported by a dedicated team ensuring exceptional pre- and post-operative care.

Emergency and Trauma Care: Our 24/7 Level I Trauma Center is equipped to handle the most critical cases, with rapid response teams and advanced life-support systems saving lives every day.
